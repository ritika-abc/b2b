Getting Started

Who all can sell their products or services on ExportersIndia.Com?

All types of manufacturers, suppliers, and sellers, be it small/medium enterprises, global corporations, or individual sellers, brands, etc., can sell their products or services on ExportersIndia.Com.

What details are required for registration on ExportersIndia.Com?

To successfully register on ExportersIndia.Com, you need:

  • Email id
  • Mobile number
  • Company Name
  • Company Address
  • At least 1 Product/Service
  • Country name

How do I register on ExportersIndia.Com?

  • Go to the homepage of ExportersIndia.Com
  • On the top left, you can find ‘Join Free’ button
  • Click on it and you will be redirected to the Registration Page.
  • Fill in the essential details like email id, name, mobile number, company name, product/service, company address, etc.
  • Click on ‘Create Account’ to successfully register.

What are the benefits of registering on ExportersIndia.Com?

ExportersIndia.Com is one of the largest business marketplaces in the world and counted among the top B2B portals in India. There are various benefits of registering here,

  • Free Registration
  • Over 14 Lakh prospective clients
  • Effective product promotion
  • Seamless Navigation
  • Strong online presence
  • Rank in organic search
  • Regularly Updated keeping the online market trends

How can ExportersIndia.Com help me in generating more business?

ExportersIndia.Com has a huge database of buyers across the globe that can help you get more leads for selling your products or services. It has over 600000 + Business Inquiry generated each month. You can search for more buyers, get recommendation of the latest buyers, post your products/services to attract leads, etc., to generate more business.

How much registration charge do I have to pay?

Registration on ExportersIndia.Com is FREE of cost.

I am unable to register on ExportersIndia.Com. What should I do?

Registration on ExportersIndia.Com is very easy. However, in case any problem arises while registration, check your email id, phone number and company name to ensure they are unique and correct. If the problem persists, write to us at our Feedback Page and we will get back to you shortly with a solution.

How secure are my business details with ExportersIndia.Com?

All the business details shared with ExportersIndia.Com are 100% safe. Our Privacy Policy has been laid down by experts to safeguard the interest of the sellers.

How much time does it take for my company to get listed on ExportersIndia.Com?

Once you have registered on ExportersIndia.Com, your company/business would get listed within 24-48 hours after verification.

Even after registration, I cannot see my company on ExportersIndia.Com. Why?

If you are not able to view your company in ExportersIndia.Com’s database, it may be because of already used/incorrect number, already used/incorrect email id, or already used/incorrect company name. Contact us at our Feedback Page for such issue.

Does ExportersIndia.Com allow more than one account for a company?

No, one company can only create one account on ExportersIndia.Com using its unique name, address, number, and email id.

Can I register more than once using the same email id?

No, you can only use one email id for a single registration.

How do I upgrade to premium membership on ExportersIndia.Com?

What is my Company Profile or Mini Catalog?

Once you have registered on ExportersIndia.Com, you get a mini catalog or a company profile where a buyer can be redirected if he/she clicks on your product/service on this portal. This mini catalog has details and images of your products/services that enable the buyer to know more about your company and its products/services.

Does ExportersIndia.Com also create a responsive website for my company?

Yes, those who take the ‘Star Package’ or the ‘Global Package’ get a responsive website for their company that is also rich with other features like water mark, live chat, and corporate video, etc.

What if I have forgotten my login password? How do I log in then?

In case you have forgotten your login password, click on ‘Forgot Password’. Enter your registered email id and password in the pop up to get a new password.
